# Privacy Policy for Doubao TTS Plugin

## Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use the Doubao Text-to-Speech (TTS) plugin. 

## Information We Collect
- **Personal Information**: We do not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it to us, such as your name or email address when you contact us.
- **Usage Data**: We may collect information about how you interact with our services, including your IP address, browser type, and operating system.

## How We Use Your Information
We may use information we collect for the following purposes:
1. To provide and maintain our services.
2. To improve, personalize, and expand our services.
3. To understand and analyze how you use our services.
4. To develop new products and services.
5. To communicate with you, either directly or through one of our partners, including for customer service, to provide you with updates and other information relating to the plugin.

## Sharing Your Information
We do not share your personal information with third parties unless required by law or with your consent.

## Data Security
We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from loss, theft, and misuse. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
